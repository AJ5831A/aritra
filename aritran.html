The Concept.

We are a brand-new (yet to debut) company. We offer the top-of-the-line green automobiles at Ariत्रा.

These are built upon the cutting-edge, effective technology created byAryan Jakhar.No more stress about charging or refueling your car. Our vehicles can self-charge using only the heat from the sun or the environment. We will modify the car to make it incredibly efficient and completely eco/pocket-friendly. The car will automatically charge itself when parked. There will be a layer of converging lenses on top of solar panels so that more sunlight will be captured this layer of lens will automatically take its position when the car is not moving.
There will be a folded extension of solar panel which will only come out when car is parked. By this extention there will be more area for solar panels to catch light which will be super helpful for fast charging. This car will be hybrid which means it can not only be charged with renewable energy but also with normal power supply.
A layer of Peltier modules will be installed in seats and steering because Peltier module is capable of generating electricity with a temperature difference by our body these modules will get the required heat and convert it into electricity which will help charge batteries. Which will increase the efficiency of the car and increase its range. The body of the car will be made with the lightest possible materials like carbon fibre and aluminum also the body will be specially streamlind to avoid air friction or the drag. These small details will be very helpful for exponential increase in efficiency.
